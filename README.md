## FAQ
### How to cancel your subscription

Subscriptions are managed by **Apple** (not directly inside FoveaReadr). To cancel:

1. Open **Settings** on your iPhone or iPad  
2. Tap **your name (Apple ID)** at the top  
3. Tap **Subscriptions**  
4. Select **FoveaReadr**  
5. Tap **Cancel Subscription** (or **Cancel Free Trial**)

You can also manage subscriptions from the **App Store**:
- Open **App Store** → tap your **profile icon** → **Subscriptions**

If you don’t see FoveaReadr listed, you may be signed in with a different Apple ID.
